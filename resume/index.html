Projects

Project Leary Engine with Vulkan from scratch

Leary is my primary side project for learning Vulkan and expanding on my low level programming skills. Everything except for font rasterisation is coded from scratch by hand in a mostly C-like subset of C++, as I find this not only enjoyable but also incredibly rewarding. This also means I am exposed to the many platform specific APIs and quirks.

Vulkan, C, C++, WinAPI, Xlib, custom preprocessor serialisation.

Cookie Attack Asteroids prototype in C++ and OpenGL 2.1

In Cookie Attack I focused on the feeling of the movement of the ship that you control while dodging and destroying asteroids (cookies), making the interaction feel right and intuitive yet challenging due to the ballistic projectile through space type of movement.

C++, OpenGL 2.1, SDL 2.0, components & entity systems

Pong Pong clone in C++ and OpenGL 2.1

For my clone of the grandfather of video games I tried to accurately, though primarily from memory and intuition, model the behaviour of the original in regards to how the ball interacts with the paddles.

C++, OpenGL 2.1, SDL 2.0
